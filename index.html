      • Click Here to visit the Repository

        • AllyIndex won 2nd prize in a hackathon organized by Code institute . This website provides Users with a central hub of information to dispel myths surrounding certain identities under the LGTBQ+ umbrella, build trust, strengthen solidarity between members of those identities and CIS gendered individuals.
        Additionally, an important feature is the quiz. Playing the quiz by answering 10 questions helps reinforce Users' new knowledge and raised awareness via 'learning through play', instead of through more tradional formal education that uses formal tests to guage learning outcomes. By incorporating 'gamification' of the User Experience, Users are encouraged to return to the website and continue to improve their score by developing their understanding of the diverse LGBTQIA+ communities.
